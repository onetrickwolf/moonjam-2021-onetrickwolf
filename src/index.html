Welcome to Dodge The Chat! Streamer has one hit and must avoid chat to save PREGARIO!

Streamer:

  • Once enough people have joined click start (would recommend 50+)
  • Left click to move, clicking further away moves faster (gets slower at higher levels)
  • Don't touch chat! One hit and it is game over! Emote is hit box (name doesn't count)
  • Save large PREGARIO (on the right side) to advance to next level

Chat:

  • During join phases: type !join [emote] to join (e.g. !join moon2CUTE) NO BetterTTV EMOTES SORRY
  • Cardinal directions (e.g. !N = move north, !SW = move south west) to move
  • Try to hit the streamer! Available commands: !N, !NE, !E, !SE, !S, !SW, !W, !NW
  • Can move only once every 3 seconds. Movement distance gets longer with each level
